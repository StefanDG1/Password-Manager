Help
You can create new entries by clicking the New Item button.
You can then click the item you want to see more information about to open a new panel.
Clicking Ctrl/Command + K will copy the currently selected entry's password into your clipboard.
To delete an entry, click it then click delete. To edit, do the same and fill information again.
You can dynamically seach for entries by title or username by using the search bar on top.
You can add groups by typing in the name of the group you want to add and clicking Add. Clicking on the newly created group will search for that group. Deleting is done by writing the group in the search bar and clicking delete.
